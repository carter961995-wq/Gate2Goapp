Need help with SafeRestore Concierge? Find answers to common questions below or contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bypass passcodes or device security?

No. SafeRestore only supports official, consent-based recovery paths. We never bypass passcodes or extract data from locked devices.

How do I start a recovery case?

Open the app, tap “Start Recovery,” and complete the intake form with device details, damage type, and ownership confirmation.

What if my device won’t turn on?

We’ll guide you to authorized repair and diagnostics while checking any available backups or synced data.

Can you help if I forgot my Apple ID password?

Yes. We guide you to Apple’s official account recovery process and track progress within your recovery plan.

Where is my data stored?

By default, data stays on your device. If you opt into concierge support, you can share specific case details with SafeRestore or authorized partners.
