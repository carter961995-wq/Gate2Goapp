Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

SafeRestore Concierge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and recovery concierge services.

What We Collect

We collect only the information needed to guide consent-based recovery, including:

  • Case details (device model, iOS version, damage type)
  • Owner-provided contact information
  • Recovery notes and progress updates you enter
  • Optional proof-of-ownership files you choose to upload

What We Do Not Do

SafeRestore does not bypass device security, extract data from locked devices, or access information without explicit owner consent. We only guide official recovery paths and authorized service channels.

Data Storage

By default, your case data is stored locally on your device. If you opt into concierge assistance, you may choose to share case data with SafeRestore or authorized service partners for support purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share information when you explicitly request support from an authorized provider, and we limit sharing to the minimum required for recovery assistance.

Security

We use device-level protections and encourage strong device passcodes. Any shared data is transmitted over secure channels and stored according to retention policies you control in the app settings.

Children's Privacy

SafeRestore is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected in the app and on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact support via the SafeRestore app.
