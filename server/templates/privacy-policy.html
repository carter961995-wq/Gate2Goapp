Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Gate2Go ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Gate2Go is designed with privacy in mind. The app stores all data locally on your device, including:

  • Project information (client names, addresses, notes)
  • Gate design specifications and pricing
  • Jobsite photos you choose to add
  • Company branding settings (logo, name, contact info)

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers. Your projects, designs, and settings remain private and under your control.

Photos

When you add jobsite photos, they are stored locally within the app on your device. We do not upload or share these photos with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Gate2Go does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. When you share a PDF proposal, you control who receives it through your device's standard sharing features.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using a passcode or biometric lock on your device to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Gate2Go is intended for professional use by contractors and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
