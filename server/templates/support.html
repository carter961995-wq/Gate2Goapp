Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?

Tap the + button on the Projects screen. Enter your client's name, address, and optionally add a jobsite photo. Tap Save to create the project.

How do I design a gate?

Open a project and you'll see the gate design workspace. Select your gate style, choose a material, enter the dimensions, and add any extras you need. The price updates automatically.

How do I save a design?

Once you're happy with your gate design and pricing, tap "Save Design" to store it in the project's gallery. You can save multiple design versions per project.

How do I generate a proposal?

Open a saved design from the gallery and tap "Generate Proposal". This creates a professional PDF with your company branding that you can share with clients.

How do I add my company logo?

Go to Settings and scroll to the Company Branding section. Tap on the logo area to select an image from your photo library.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Your projects, designs, and settings are private and never uploaded to any servers.
